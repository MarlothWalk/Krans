Umtoma ( Jackalberry)
Inombolo yekucala. [ ngembi kwalapha kupakwa khona timoto]
Inchazelo :  Umtoma sihlahla lesingakhula ngemamitha lalinganiselwa kumashumi lamabili, kuya etulu  kantsi budze baso bulinganiselwa  kumamitha lalishumi nesihlanu.  
Indalo yaso: Lesihlahla sidzinga indzawo lenotsile futsi letsambile ngasemifuleni . Tilwane letifana ne nyalas, timphala, tindayikazane, tingobiyane  kanye nenyoni lendzala yemhlaba kudla lesitselo semtoma.
Emaphuzu lachazako: Imbewu nobe lenhlanyelo Yemtoma itfolakala ebulongweni aba Jakalazi, kungako icanjwe leligama.  Sihlahla salapha eNingizimu Africa lesikunombolo  606 nobe emakhulu laitfupha nesitfupha..
